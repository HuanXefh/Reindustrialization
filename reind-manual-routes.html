Reind: Production Routes


This document contains steps involved in most routes. Products are listed in alphabetical order.

If a row is colored to #fed37f, the step is optional.

If a row is colored to #808080, the step is alternative to the previous step.

If the name of a factory is colored to #cbd97f, it represents a factory family (there multiple factories available to carry out the same step).
